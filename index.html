How I Work

My Working Process

1.

Understanding
& Research

I start by reviewing the project requirements, client instructions, or school task objectives. I research similar websites, design ideas, and technical solutions to understand what will work best.

2.

Planning &
Structuring

Before coding, I outline the layout, pages, and features needed. I decide what tools, plugins, or technologies to use and organize the workflow so development becomes smoother.

3.

Building &
Testing
